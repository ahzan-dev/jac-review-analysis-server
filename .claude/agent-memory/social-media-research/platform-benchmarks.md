## Platform-Specific Content Style Guidelines
### Twitter/X
- Hook in first 70 chars
- 1-2 hashtags max
- Conversational/punchy tone
- Quote tweet to drive thread engagement
- Numbers and data perform well ("3 reasons why...")

### Instagram
- First 125 chars = critical preview text
- Emoji-friendly (2-4 emojis per post optimal)
- 9-11 relevant hashtags
- Carousel format for listicles/tips/comparisons
- Visual CTA ("double tap if..." "tag a friend who...")

### Facebook
- Shorter is better (80 chars for highest ER)
- Community-focused language
- Questions drive comments
- 1-2 hashtags only
- Native video > link shares

### LinkedIn
- First 210 chars on desktop = critical preview
- Professional but personal storytelling
- Subject-matter expertise signals
- Poll posts drive high engagement
- Longer form content OK (stories, lessons learned)
- 3-5 professional/industry hashtags
- Replying to comments boosts post engagement 30%

### TikTok
- 15-60 second sweet spot
- Hook in first 2 seconds (critical for algorithm)
- Trending audio boosts reach
- Text overlay for accessibility
- UGC format (2.4x more authentic perception)
- Serialized content / series format works well

### Google Business Profile
- Event/offer posts for local SEO signals
- Include location keywords naturally
- Strong CTA with Google's built-in buttons
- Photos: 1200Ã—900px minimum
- Weekly posts signal active business

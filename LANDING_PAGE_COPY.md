## 14. DESIGN & LAYOUT RECOMMENDATIONS

### Visual Hierarchy

1. **Hero section** - Bold, attention-grabbing. Your best headline should be massive (60-80px)
2. **Feature sections** - Scannable. Use icons, whitespace, short headlines. Readers should get the gist in 3 seconds of scanning
3. **Testimonials** - Personal, real. Use actual customer photos (not stock). Include specific metrics
4. **Pricing** - Crystal clear. Use visual anchoring (highlight "most popular" tier in different color)
5. **FAQ** - Expandable accordion format. Show only headlines; expand on click

### Color & Imagery

**Recommended Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep blue or green (trust, growth, stability)
- Accent: Orange or coral (action, energy, CTA buttons)
- Neutral: White, light gray (breathing room)
- Text: Dark gray or black (readability)

**Imagery:**
- Avoid: Stock photos of people in business suits, generic "success" images
- Use: Screenshots of actual reports, real customer photos, data visualizations, before/after comparisons
- Charts/graphs showing trends, health scores, improvements

### CTA Button Style

- **Primary CTA**: Full-width or prominent button with accent color. Clear, readable text.
- **Secondary CTA**: Outline style or smaller. Less dominant than primary.
- **Hover state**: Slight color shift, shadow, or scale effect
- **Loading state**: Spinner or progress indication

## Quick Wins (Low Effort, High Impact)

These features provide maximum value with minimal development time:

- **PDF Export** - Most requested feature for sharing with stakeholders
- **CSV/Excel Export** - Essential for power users and data analysts
- **Email Alerts** - Keeps users engaged and informed
- **Response Template Library** - Accelerates reply workflow
- **Executive Summary Mode** - Perfect for busy business owners
- **Free Trial** - Reduces friction for new user acquisition
- **Review Language Translation** - Opens international market
- **Emotion Intensity Scoring** - Differentiates from competitors

## Strategic Investments (High Effort, High Impact)

Major features requiring significant development but with transformative potential:

- **Multi-Source Reviews** - Becomes single source of truth for all reviews
- **Team Accounts** - Unlocks B2B enterprise sales
- **Competitive Benchmarking** - Creates "must-have" differentiation
- **Competitor Monitoring** - Drives daily engagement and retention
- **Google Business API** - Enables direct reply publishing workflow
- **Root Cause Analysis** - Premium AI feature justifying higher pricing
- **Auto Reply Publishing** - Full automation closes the feedback loop
- **CRM Integration** - Enterprise requirement for large organizations
